Young stars and disks

I study young stars and their protoplanetary disks to learn more about star and planet formation. My Ph.D. thesis involves using optical data and sub-millimeter interferometric data. We recently developed a novel technique for inferring stellar properties.

Energetic transients

Previously, I worked with Dr. Edo Berger to identify and characterize exotic transients found by the Pan-STARRS survey, including supernovae, classical novae, GRBs, and stellar outbursts. I developed multi-wavelength observing expertise from the X-ray to the radio.
