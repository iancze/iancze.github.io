# SAMSI Exoplanet Workshop

In October 2016, I joined many statistically-oriented exoplaneteers at the *Hierarchical Bayesian Modeling of Exoplanet Populations* workshop at the Statistical and Applied Mathematics Institute (SAMSI) in North Carolina. I wrote a post for the [SAMSI blog](https://samsiatrtp.wordpress.com/2016/11/14/astro-workshop-brings-researchers-together-to-discuss-exoplanet-exploration/) summarizing my experience as well as highlighting some of the projects that I found most interesting. You can find the whole post **[here](https://samsiatrtp.wordpress.com/2016/11/14/astro-workshop-brings-researchers-together-to-discuss-exoplanet-exploration/)**. Thank you to the organizers Angie Wolfgang (Penn State), Eric Ford (Penn State), and Sujit Ghosh (NC State)!
